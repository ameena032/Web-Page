With joy of colors in them,

But...In the society?

Is she allowed to fly away like one?
Does she have the permission to

No!then why this comparison?
Even if forsaken,
Then why dont we let her be the butterfly
Of our society?

Let a girl be independent,
Let her fly high
let her get rid of any sigh,
